Cheese Toast Receipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 plain long buns
  • Butter
  • Mayonaise
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather alll your indgredients into one spot
  2. get a clean empty bowl
  3. add 1 table spoon of butter
  4. add 1 table spoon of mayonaise
  5. add grated cheese
  6. mix well until all indgredients are mushed well together
  7. preheat oven for 15 minutes at 180 c
  8. bake for 3-5 mins until golden brown
